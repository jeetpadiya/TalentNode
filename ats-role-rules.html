ATS Roles and Rules

MVP role definition for TalentNode Application Tracking System.

Core Idea

  • Candidate: applies to jobs and tracks their own applications.
  • Recruiter: manages jobs, applications, and hiring pipeline.
  • Hiring Manager: reviews candidates and participates in hiring decisions.
  • Interviewer: accesses assigned interviews and submits interview feedback.
  • Admin: manages organization-wide users, jobs, permissions, and settings.

Candidate Rules

  • Can sign up publicly.
  • Can view public and open jobs.
  • Can apply to jobs.
  • Can view and update only their own profile.
  • Can view only their own applications.
  • Cannot create jobs.
  • Cannot view other candidates.
  • Cannot move application stages.
  • Cannot manage team members or organization settings.

Recruiter Rules

  • Can create, edit, publish, and archive jobs.
  • Can view all jobs in their organization.
  • Can view all applications in their organization.
  • Can move applications through pipeline stages.
  • Can add notes to candidates and applications.
  • Can shortlist or reject candidates.
  • Can schedule interviews.
  • Can view candidate profiles tied to organization jobs.
  • Cannot manage global platform settings.

Hiring Manager Rules

  • Can view jobs they own or are assigned to.
  • Can view applicants for assigned jobs.
  • Can review candidate profiles and resumes.
  • Can leave feedback and notes.
  • Can move candidates between limited stages based on business rules.
  • Can approve or reject hiring decisions if your process allows it.
  • Cannot access unrelated jobs by default.
  • Cannot manage organization users unless explicitly allowed.

Interviewer Rules

  • Can view only interviews assigned to them.
  • Can view limited candidate details for assigned interviews.
  • Can submit interview feedback.
  • Can submit recommendations such as strong_yes, yes, no, or strong_no.
  • Cannot create or edit jobs.
  • Cannot move the full application pipeline by default.
  • Cannot view all candidate or organization data.

Admin Rules

  • Has full access inside their organization.
  • Can manage users and assign roles.
  • Can create, edit, and archive all jobs.
  • Can view all applications.
  • Can manage pipeline settings.
  • Can manage organization settings.
  • Can view reports and dashboard data.
  • Can invite, deactivate, and reset staff accounts.
  • Should be the only role allowed to promote others to admin.

Data Access Rules

  • Every staff role must be scoped to organizationId.
  • Candidates must never access another candidate's data.
  • Interviewers should only access assigned interviews and candidate records.
  • Hiring managers should only access jobs they own or are assigned to.
  • Recruiters and admins can access organization-wide jobs and applications.

MVP Middleware Recommendations

  • authenticateUser
  • authorizeRoles("admin")
  • authorizeRoles("recruiter", "admin")
  • authorizeRoles("hiring_manager", "recruiter", "admin")
  • authorizeRoles("interviewer", "hiring_manager", "recruiter", "admin")
